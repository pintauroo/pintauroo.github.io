Hi. I'm Andrea Pinto.

andrea.pinto.1@slu.edu

Andrea Pinto is a driven Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering with a passion for computer networks, and artificial intelligence. His primary adviser is the renowned Dr. Flavio Esposito, and he is currently conducting research in the field of network softwarization through technologies such as Software-Defined Networks. Prior to beginning his doctoral studies, Andrea earned his Bachelor's and Master's Degrees in Computer Engineering from the University of Naples, Federico II, Italy, in 2020. During his Master's, he worked as a thesis worker at Ericsson Research Lab in Helsinki, Finland. Before that, he worked as a Software Engineer at Ericsson in Italy. With his wealth of experience in the field, Andrea is sure to make an impact in the world of Computer Engineering.
