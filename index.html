Hi. I'm Andrea Pinto.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Saint Louis University, advised by Dr. Flavio Esposito.

My research focuses on distributed training and bandwidth-aware orchestration to alleviate network bottlenecks. I try to make systems perform better, deeply understaning the problems and how to solve them

I received my M.S. and B.S. in Computer Engineering from the University of Naples, Federico II. During my Master's, I was a research intern at Ericsson Research in Helsinki, where I wrote my thesis on integrating heterogeneous IoT ecosystems. Before my Ph.D., I worked as a Cloud Engineer at Ericsson in Italy, contributing to the company's shift towards microservice-based cloud architectures.
